Soccer Murder

  • High Concept: Collect the evidence and select the killer
  • Player Experience Goals: The player will be forced to use intuition and problem solving abilities.
  • Genre: This game is puzzle/stragety.
  • Game play: Control cop with arrow keys, then use mouse to select the correct crook.
  • Prototype goal: Incorperating sound and clicking and killing pictures.
  • Features: Game fetures include sound effects, clicking, arrow keys.
  • Setting/Premise: At the World Cup a player was murdered and you need to find the killer.
  • Story: Superstar soccer player Cristiano Ronaldo was found dead at the World Cup. Police have to collect some evidence and figure out who the killer is. Each piece of evidence will lead you closer to the killer.The suspects are Thierry Henry, Clint Dempsey, David Beckham, and Miroslav Klose. Collect the evidence, find the Killer and Solve the case.
  • Target Audience: Casual gamers.
  • Play time: Not very long but has mutliple outcomes.
  • Strategies: Have a prior knowledge of soccer.
  • Prototype Assets: All of the pictures were found with google searches and then resized and photoshoped/edited.
